ARV7 AIR CONDITIONING SYSTEM

ARV7 is the latest generation system from AUX. Highly advanced technologically, these devices are the perfect solution for commercial, office, hotel, and residential buildings (ARV Mini).


A wide range of indoor units and outdoor modules, which can be configured into systems with a capacity of up to 404 kW, allows for the selection of the right solution for any facility and the implementation of even the most unusual and demanding installations.

Revolutionary VER Technology

The evaporation and condensation temperature of the refrigerant significantly affects the cooling and heating performance as well as the energy efficiency of the air conditioning system.

Thanks to the use of revolutionary Variable Energy Efficiency Ratio (VER) technology, the outdoor units of the ARV7 series feature different operating modes with varying refrigerant temperatures – the basic mode (default), turbo mode, and high-efficiency mode. This allows for flexible adjustment of the system's performance and energy efficiency to match the current cooling or heating demand, thereby enabling control over the operating costs of the air conditioning system.

High EER and COP Coefficients

Thanks to the exclusive use of high-efficiency DC inverter compressors with enhanced vapor injection technology, all ARV7 series devices achieve the highest energy efficiency ratios in their class for both cooling and heating operation.

180˚ Sinusoidal Control Technology

Thanks to the exclusive use of high-efficiency DC inverter compressors with enhanced vapor injection technology, all ARV7 series devices achieve the highest energy efficiency ratios in their class for both cooling and heating operation.

Brushless Fan Motor

The brushless DC motor adjusts the fan speed according to system pressure and workload to increase efficiency by 45%. The Super Aero fan ensures greater air volume and higher static pressure.

High-Efficiency Motors

High-efficiency magnetic motors enhance the performance of traditional DC inverter compressors.

High-Efficiency Heat Exchanger

The optimised 2-to-1 refrigeration circuit design enhances heat exchange efficiency and improves the ratio of liquid flowing to the evaporator.

FEATURES AND FUNCTIONS

HEALTH

Fresh air intake

The possibility of supplying fresh air to the indoor unit. This solution improves indoor air quality, positively affecting the health and comfort of users.

Durable air filter

The indoor unit's air filter can be easily removed and cleaned. It is resistant to repeated cleaning, significantly extending its lifespan.

Self-cleaning function

Thanks to the comprehensive self-cleaning function, the interior of the air conditioner is kept clean by eliminating moisture and inhibiting the growth of bacteria, mold, and unpleasant odors on the heat exchanger. This ensures a clean and safe environment for users.

COMFORT

Anti-Cold-Air Function

When starting operation in heating mode, the fan of the indoor unit operates at the lowest speed to avoid blowing cold air. The airflow speed is automatically increased as the evaporator temperature rises, up to the set value.

Follow me Function

The temperature sensor built into the wireless controller allows for measuring the current temperature in the air-conditioned space. The measurement result is sent to the indoor unit, which then automatically adjusts the operating parameters to the conditions in the room.

Fast cooling / heating

Thanks to the use of inverter technology, the air conditioning system can quickly reach full capacity after startup and ensure the rapid attainment of the desired room temperature.

Independent dehumidification

Thanks to the independent dehumidification function, the air conditioner can reduce the humidity level in the room to an optimal value, enhancing the comfort of the users.

Silent mode

In silent mode, the indoor unit of the air conditioner operates with a reduced sound pressure level, providing comfortable conditions in the room without unnecessary noise.

3D airflow function

The automatic movement of the louvers in both the vertical and horizontal planes allows for the rapid distribution of air in all directions.

Auto Auto-Swing louvre movement

The air louvers in the indoor unit move automatically in the horizontal plane – left and right (horizontal swing) or in the vertical plane – up and down (vertical swing), ensuring even distribution of the blown air throughout the room

Display activation

Pressing the "Dimmer" button on the controller will turn off the display on the front panel of the indoor unit.

ENERGY EFFICIENCY

Night mode

When the sleep function is activated, the device increases the set temperature by 1°C per hour during the first two hours in cooling mode (or decreases it at the same rate in heating mode). For the next five hours, it maintains the achieved temperature at a constant level, after which the air conditioner switches off.

180° sine wave control technology

The DC inverter compressor uses 180° sine wave control technology, ensuring smooth motor operation and a significant increase in efficiency compared to traditional technology. It also contributes to a lower noise level emitted by the outdoor units.

DC inverter technology

DC control, DC compressor, and DC indoor and outdoor unit fan. The use of inverter technology significantly reduces energy consumption compared to standard devices.

Fins coated with a hydrophilic layer

Fins coated with a hydrophilic layer increase efficiency by 10% and improve condensation speed.

RELIABILITY

Self-diagnosis function

The device monitors malfunctions and shuts down in the event of a failure. An error code indicating the type of fault appears on the indoor unit display (or wired controller), significantly simplifying service procedures.

Cooling at low temperatures

Top-quality components, such as a high-efficiency compressor and a premium heat exchanger, ensure reliable air conditioner operation in cooling mode even at very low outdoor temperatures.

Frost-resistant design

The unique heat exchanger design ensures a higher casing temperature, preventing water accumulation from the defrosting process. This increases heat exchange efficiency and eliminates drainage issues.

Gold-coated heat exchanger fins

The unique gold coating on the heat exchanger surface effectively prevents bacterial growth and improves the unit's efficiency. It also provides effective protection against corrosion, extending the device's lifespan.

Fire-resistant electrical box

The device is equipped with a highly fire-resistant electrical box, preventing the risk of fire in the event of an internal short circuit.

Compressor crankcase heater

An additional heating belt raises the compressor oil temperature in winter and prevents water from freezing, improving heat exchange efficiency.

Intelligent defrosting

The intelligent defrosting system ensures nearly uninterrupted operation of the air conditioner in heating mode. The defrost mode is activated only when necessary and only for the time required to defrost the heat exchanger, allowing for more efficient unit operation while heating the room.

CONVENIENCE

24-hour timer

The timer function allows the user to set the exact time for the air conditioner to turn on and off automatically.

Built-in condensate pump

The device is equipped with a high-lift condensate pump.

Two-way condensate drainage

The condensate drainage system can be connected from either the left or right side of the indoor unit, significantly simplifying installation.

Digital display

The digital display panel shows the current operating mode of the device. Parameters such as room temperature and operating mode are clearly displayed in real time.

Washable air filter

The indoor unit's air filter can be easily removed and cleaned under running water, helping to maintain high device efficiency and air quality.

Automatic restart

In the event of a power outage, the device remembers the last settings and automatically resumes operation with the previously set mode once power is restored.

CONTROL

Wireless controller

The wireless remote control allows for convenient operation of the air conditioner, including turning the device on and off, selecting settings and functions such as operating mode, airflow temperature, fan speed, and airflow direction.

Wired controller

The wired controller is installed on the wall in the room where the air conditioner is located, allowing for easy and convenient operation of the unit.

Central controller

The central controller allows for managing multiple air conditioning systems, enabling control over a larger number of indoor units.

Wi-Fi control module

The Wi-Fi module allows the air conditioner to be controlled via a smartphone, tablet, or computer connected to the internet from anywhere in the world.

The ARV7 series has obtained the prestigious Eurovent certification, which guarantees the declared parameters are met.
