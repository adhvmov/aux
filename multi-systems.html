MULTI SPLIT SYSTEM

Flexible air conditioning solutions for multiple rooms

The multi split AUX system allows the installation of 2 to 5 indoor units to one outdoor unit. The offer includes 7 models of outdoor units providing cooling capacity from 4.1 to 12.0 kW. The devices operate using ecological R32 refrigerant and modern inverter technology, providing reliability and energy efficiency. They offer stable operation at a wide range of ambient temperatures, ranging from -15 ° C to 52 ° C in cooling mode and from -22°C to 24°C in heating mode. The system allows the design of cooling systems with a total length of up to 70 meters, depending on the power of the compressor and level differences up to 10 meters.

MULTI SPLIT SYSTEM

Space saving and high flexibility

The multi split AUX system allows you to connect 2 to 5 indoor units to one outdoor unit. It reduces the number of outdoor units in comparison with single systems and thus offers a significant space saving on building facades, roofs and balconies. In addition, the devices do not have to be installed at the same time – the system can be expanded with new indoor units over time, maintaining the appropriate performance parameters of individual parts of the air conditioning system.

A WIDE RANGE OF INDOOR UNITS

Always a good fitting to the space

The AUX multi-series offers a range of 10 types of indoor units – cassette, ducted, floor-ceiling, console-type, and three models of stylish wall-mounted units (Freedom series, J-Smart and J-Smart ART, Q-Smart Premium, Q-Smart Premium Grey, C-Smart). These units are available in various cooling capacities, ranging from 2.05 to 7.03 kW. Such a wide selection allows for a good match of units to the size and characteristics of the room as well as the needs of the users.

HIGH QUALITY INVERTER COMPRESSOR

Guarantee of energy efficiency and reliability

The inverter rotary compressor of AUX air conditioners guarantees reliable and efficient operation of the devices as well as the highest energy efficiency parameters. It also provides low operating costs and a low level of noise. Durable and high-quality materials of the compressor guarantee its long and failure-free operation.

R32 REFRIGERANT

Ecological, highly effective, safe

All AUX air conditioning units of CAC series operate using the newest ecological refrigerant R32, which, comparing to the commonly used R41OA refrigerant, is characterized by higher efficiency and a lower greenhouse effect. It also does not damage the ozone layer.

HIGH ENERGY EFFICIENCY

Guarantee of energy-saving operation

Owing to the use of a highly efficient DC inverter compressor, adjustable fan motor and advanced 180 ° sine wave vector control technology, commercial AUX systems achieve high energy efficiency parameters, providing reliable, efficient operation with low energy consumption. All CAC air conditioners are class A ++ in cooling and A + in heating mode.

A wide range of operation

Reliable operation even in adverse weather conditions

AUX commercial air conditioning systems are characterized by reliable, stable operation even in extreme ambient temperatures. Owing to the use of modern technologies and high-quality components, AUX devices operate in a wide range of outdoor temperatures, i.e. from -15 ° C to 52 ° C in cooling mode and from -22°C to 24°C in heating mode, providing a high level of comfort in the room in all the seasons of the year.

MULTI SPLIT SYSTEM

Quiet Operation

Experience the comfort of whisper-quiet operation with our multi-split systems. The advanced noise reduction technology ensures minimal sound levels both indoors and outdoors. The indoor units operate at as low as 19 dB, while the outdoor units feature specially designed fan blades and vibration dampening systems for reduced noise emission.

MULTI SPLIT SYSTEM

Smart Air Quality Control

Our multi-split systems are equipped with advanced air quality control features. The built-in air purification system removes dust, allergens, and harmful particles from the air. The humidity control system maintains optimal indoor humidity levels, while the ionizer helps neutralize airborne bacteria and viruses for healthier indoor air quality.

MULTI SPLIT SYSTEM

Energy Saving Mode

The intelligent energy-saving mode automatically adjusts the system's operation to minimize power consumption while maintaining comfort. The system learns your usage patterns and optimizes its performance accordingly. With the ECO mode, you can reduce energy consumption by up to 30% while still enjoying optimal comfort.

MULTI SPLIT SYSTEM

Durable Construction

Built with premium materials and robust construction, our multi-split systems are designed for long-lasting performance. The outdoor units feature corrosion-resistant coatings and weather-resistant components, while the indoor units are constructed with high-quality materials that resist wear and tear. This durability ensures reliable operation for years to come.

MULTI SPLIT SYSTEM

Easy Maintenance

Our multi-split systems are designed with user-friendly maintenance in mind. The washable filters are easily accessible and removable, while the self-cleaning function helps prevent mold and bacteria buildup. The system's diagnostic features make it easy to identify and address any potential issues, ensuring hassle-free maintenance and optimal performance.

MULTI SPLIT SYSTEM

Advanced Control Technology

Our multi-split systems feature advanced control technology that allows for precise temperature management and energy optimization. The smart control system enables individual room temperature settings, scheduling, and remote control capabilities through mobile applications. This technology ensures optimal comfort while maximizing energy efficiency.
